Best Practices
For Better Recognition

  • ✓ Good natural or bright lighting

  • ✓ Hair should not cover face
  • ✓ Natural facial expressions
  • ✓ Various angles (10-20 degrees)
Avoid
  • ✗ Sunglasses or dark glasses
  • ✗ Hats or head covers
  • ✗ Low lighting or shadows
  • ✗ Side profiles only
