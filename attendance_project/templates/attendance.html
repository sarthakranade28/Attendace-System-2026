Camera Instructions
Important
  • Make sure the model is trained with student data
  • Position your face clearly in the camera frame
  • Good lighting is essential for accurate recognition
  • The system will automatically mark recognized faces
  • Press ESC on the keyboard to stop capturing
Do's
  • Face the camera directly
  • Keep face in center of frame
  • Use natural lighting
  • Keep eyes open & visible
  • Calm, neutral expression
Don'ts
  • Don't wear sunglasses
  • Don't cover face with hands
  • Avoid side profiles only
  • No extreme head tilts
  • Don't have blurry images
