Frequently Asked Questions
Q: How long does training take?

A: Training typically takes 1-3 minutes for 5-10 students. More data = longer training time.

Q: What if training fails?

A: Check console for errors. Common issues: corrupted images, no registered students, or insufficient memory.

Q: Can I retrain with new data?

A: Yes! Register new students and click Train again. Old models will be overwritten.

Q: Can I train only selected students?

A: Yes! Select the students you want to train using the checkboxes above, then click "Start Training".
